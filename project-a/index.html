Mind: Universe

Scientific Name:

Mindy

Discovered by / at:

Everyone who wants to figure out what happens in their mind.

Family:

The little stars in the background

Habitat:

Mindy will react to the stimulations given by the surroundings, whose reponse is to change its color and size.

Appearance:

Flower like and star like.

Key Characteristics:

  • Changeable size and color
  • Soft
  • Different shapes

What do we know so far about the creature?

It's the concrete image of one's mind and thoughts, showing how we're reacting to and communicate with the world. It lives in the universe of one's mind, and can be different in different people's brains, however the interactions it has with the outside world remain the same. It gains power from those little inspirations, and can be stimulated by the outside.

How do we interact with this creature?

Give it a touch, then it'll change its color and shape.
